what is access bars?

Bars are 32 points on your head and there we store the thoughts, ideas, beliefs, emotions, and considerations that we base our life on.

Access Bars can feel like hitting the delete button on your computer’s cluttered hard drive – only this time, you’re creating space in your brain. When gently touched, effortlessly and easily release things like negative thought patterns, or that endless mental chatter keeping you awake at night that stops you from creating a life you love. But once released, space for the calm you’ve been seeking is created.
